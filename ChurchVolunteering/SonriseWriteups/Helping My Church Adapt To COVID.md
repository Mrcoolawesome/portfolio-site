When COVID hit, our Church at the time had to change from fully in-person to being fully online. This was a huge change for everyone, especially those not very tech literate. 

I offered to help record, edit, and publish the Sunday sermons as YouTube videos, and my pastor gladly accepted. I then went on to post sermons that lasted ~1hr long every week on time, being fully edited to include captions for the worship section, slides for the sermon, and any other cuts or color correction needed. 

Through this experience I learned a lot about how to consistently hit deadlines, how to work with a large team to get a production done, and even learned how to use Blender to make the intro for the videos.
